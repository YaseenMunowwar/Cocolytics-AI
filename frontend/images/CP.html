Coconut Price Forecasting Concept Map

Key Findings from the Literature

  • Traditional statistical models like ARIMA provide a strong baseline but machine learning approaches (ANNs, LSTM, SVR) often demonstrate superior performance with lower error metrics.
  • Hybrid models that combine linear and nonlinear techniques (e.g., ARIMA-NARNET) show promising results, capturing both linear trends and nonlinear patterns.
  • Coconut price forecasting is essential for multiple stakeholders: farmers, traders, processors, policymakers, and consumers.
  • Main data sources in Sri Lanka include the Coconut Development Authority, Department of Census and Statistics, and Central Bank of Sri Lanka.
  • Future research should address climate change impacts, data limitations, model interpretability, and integration with decision systems.
